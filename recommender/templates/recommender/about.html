About AlignEd

Guiding Students Towards a Brighter Future with AI

Our Mission

Choosing a college course is one of the most significant decisions a student will make. At AlignEd, our mission is to simplify this process for the students of Ninoy Aquino Senior High School. We believe that by leveraging the power of Artificial Intelligence, we can provide personalized, data-driven recommendations that align a student's unique skills, interests, and abilities with the best possible academic path. Our goal is to empower students, reduce uncertainty, and help them take the first confident step towards a successful and fulfilling career.

How It Works

1. Take the Assessment

Answer a series of engaging, multi-step questions about your interests and abilities.

2. AI Analysis

Our Random Forest model analyzes your unique profile against patterns from hundreds of data points.

3. Get Recommendations

Receive your top 3 course recommendations, complete with personalized insights and visualizations.

The Development Team

This project was developed as a capstone requirement by a dedicated group of students passionate about technology and education.
